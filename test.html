Men's Suicide Epidemic Awareness

About the Issue

Men's mental health and suicide rates are a pressing concern. This website aims to raise awareness and provide resources to address this issue.

Understanding the Problem

Men often face societal pressures and stigmas that discourage seeking help for mental health challenges. This can contribute to higher suicide rates.

Get Involved

If you or someone you know is struggling, it's important to reach out for support. You are not alone.

Resources

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: Call 1-800-273-TALK (1-800-273-8255) for help 24/7.
Crisis Text Line: Text "HELLO" to 741741 for free, confidential support.
Your Local Mental Health Services: Find local resources and organizations that provide mental health support.
